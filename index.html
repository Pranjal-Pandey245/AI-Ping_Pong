Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away
  3. Move your right wrist in ascending or decending manner, red dot should appear on your right wrist
  4. Press the play button
  5. The red paddel is your paddel, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist
